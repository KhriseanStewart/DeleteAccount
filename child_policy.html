FOMII - Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation

FOMII is committed to creating a safe and respectful environment for all users, especially minors. We strictly prohibit any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

Our Policy

  • We do not tolerate any content or behavior that promotes, facilitates, or condones CSAE.
  • We actively monitor and review content and user activity to identify and remove any violations.
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement authorities in investigating and prosecuting offenders.
  • We implement age-appropriate safety measures to protect minors and ensure compliance with applicable laws.

Our Commitments

  • Regularly updating our safety policies and measures.
  • Educating our users about safe online practices.
  • Providing mechanisms for users to report concerns related to CSAE.
  • Ensuring prompt action against violations, including account suspension or termination.
